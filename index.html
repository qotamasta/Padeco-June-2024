This is PADECO

Always Participatory

OUR VISION

Is to have a self-determined, empowered and developed society of Tanzania.

OUR MISSION

Is to promote, support, sustain socio-economic programs and address emergency as well as relief services within and outside PADECO areas of operation.

OUR MAIN GOAL

Is to improve the literacy, eradicate absolute poverty, create job opportunities and build disciplinary self-reliant communities with improved living standards.

Our Values

These are deeply ingrained principles that guide all of PADECO's actions; they serve as the cultural cornerstones.

We always strive to do the right thing and to make a positive difference in the lives of those we serve.

We value everyone and treat people with dignity and professionalism.

Each of us is responsible for our words, our actions, and our results.

We build trust through responsible actions and honest relationships.

We are always act in good faith.

Whether things are good or bad, we openly and honestly convey information as we know it. Our egos are not involved.

We achieve more when we collaborate and all work together.

What We Do

Water Supply

Having clean water and sanitation means being able to avoid exposure to countless diseases. Hence we have implemented several projects on this; From Ugera and Mhumbi-Lipangala (Ludewa), to Lwangu (Njombe) and more.

Education improvement

From provision of scholarships to construction of new schools, school facilities and renovations.

Support to Entrepreneurship

From provision of vocational tools and trainings to supporting youth entrepreneurship groups.

Environmental conservation

From water catchments protection and tree planting programs to promotion of sustainable energy sources.

Health and diseases

We foster better health by promoting healthy nutrition, providing facilities, raising awareness on HIV/AIDS and COVID-19.

Human rights, gender and child protection

"Everyone, everywhere has the same rights as a result of our common humanity. We are all equally entitled to our human rights without discrimination."
